    Preface

    In the light of the mountainous amount of data being processed real time around the world and that too with a few milli seconds for processing, comes in the relevance of a high performance database.

    Second only to high frequency trading when it comes to low latency, real-time bidding platforms have fewer than 100 milliseconds to win a bid and serve an ad. To build a competitive offering, these systems have to store context on billions of users and devices and process queries and updates within a few milliseconds. Therefore, front-edge operational database use is overwhelmingly key-value, with models and insights generated using offline systems (e.g., Hadoop).

    This meet up hence showcases the technical system architecture of a high performance database which features availability, high performance, clustering, Flash storage, and immediate consistency
