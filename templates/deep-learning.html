    Introduction

    This year, we're hosting India's first deep learning conference on 2nd July, bringing together practitioners, researchers and developers to understand conceptual issues in Deep Learning, how Deep Learning is emerging as a distinct discipline within the field of Machine Learning, and to review cutting edge technological developments in India and across the world.

    The conference will have full, crisp and lightning talks from 10AM to 5PM, followed by two parallel tracks of workshops on 3rd July. The workshops will cover NLP in the first track and Computer Vision / AI in the second. Participants can join the track of their choice on July 3rd. Workshop tickets have to be purchased separately.
