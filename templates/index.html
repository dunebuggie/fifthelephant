    Introduction

    3 days - 50+ - speakers - 1000+ smartest programmers,
    data scientists and CTOs.
    Descend in Namma Bengaluru's most happening
    Community powered conference & workshops
    on large-scale data processing.

    The Fifth Elephant is India’s most renowned data science conference. It is a space for discussing some of the most cutting edge developments in the fields of machine learning, data science and technology that powers data collection and analysis.

    The Fifth Elephant 2016 will revolve around Machine Learning, Distributed and Parallel Computing, and High-performance Computing. This year's edition is being held on 28th and 29th July, at the NIMHANS Convention Centre, Bangalore

    View Funnel Submissions

    This year we are organising a Deep learning conference as part of the lead-up to fifth elephant. It will have exploratory talks in the morning and workshops post-lunch, running on two parallel tracks -- on applying deep learning to Natural Language Processing and Image Processing.

    For more updates, follows us on Facebook and Twitter.
