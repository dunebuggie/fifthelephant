    The Fifth Elephant is India’s most renowned conference on data, a space for discussing the most relevant tools, techniques and frameworks with expert practitioners in the field.

    Fifth Elephant 2016 will be centered around machine learning and distributed/parallel/high-performance computing. It will happen on 28th and 29th July, at the NIMHANS Convention Centre, Bangalore.

    Conference Highlights

    Foursquare is a popular app; Chipotle, a successful restaurant chain. Foursquare used the drop in walk-ins by its users into Chipotle to predict a 30% fall in Chipotle's earnings -- to the last percentage point. This is the power of data, even -- or especially -- ‘alternative’ data.

    The advent of Machine Learning has been described as the second industrial revolution. Join the vanguard at this years’ edition of The Fifth Elephant: we’ll be looking at where and how ML has penetrated the marketplace and into domains like fintech, finance, advertising, e-commerce, health and education.

    We'll also look at handling data at scale, and handling it in real time, and the challenges of bringing realtime logging, realtime analysis, and the processing of massive datasets into production. Not least, we've got to know how good our data is - so we'll go over the best benchmarking tools, versioning and how best to handle large data for business.

    If you're getting your hands dirty as an engineer or analyzing complex datasets, optimizing returns across your business or looking for optimal returns for your capital, join us at The Fifth Elephant. If you'd prefer to get a longer, more hands-on experience, consider attending our workshops.
